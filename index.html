VISUALIZING BIRD MIGRATION PATTERNS

Birds are essential to ecology and travel thousands of miles across continents. Their migration is a visible testimony to nature’s self-balancing patterns. Birds adapt by flying to safe sanctuaries abroad when they cannot bear the harsh environment in their home country. Studying bird migration cues what’s happening in the environment. We are visualizing bird migration patterns from 2015 to 2019 for some species of birds.
